Jennie Kim

K-pop singer of BlackPink/ Solo artist

PROFILE

Jennie Kim is a South Korean singer and rapper, best known as a member of BLACKPINK. Born on January 16, 1996, she studied in New Zealand and is fluent in English. Jennie debuted in 2016 and became famous for her strong stage presence and unique style.

WORK EXPERIENCE

Jennie Kim began her career in 2016 as a member of BLACKPINK under YG Entertainment, quickly gaining global fame through music, concerts, and world tours. In 2018, she started her solo career with the hit song “SOLO.” She has also worked as a model and global ambassador for luxury brand Chanel, showing her influence in fashion.

SKILLS

Singing, Rapping, Dancing, Stage performance / charisma, Bilingual (Korean & English), Songwriting, Acting and Fashion sense (works with Chanel).

EDUCATION

Jennie Kim received part of her education in New Zealand. She studied at ACG Parnell College, where she lived for several years. During this time, she became fluent in English and experienced an international education before returning to South Korea to pursue her music career.
